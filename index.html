Hello! My name is Jeremiah Nyman

My passion is understanding how people think and why they make the decisions that they do.

I began my journey into Psychology back in 2010, where I started learning behavioral psychology at Wichita State University.

After graduating college in 2012, I knew that my journey into User Experience wasn't complete, and the next path was to learn more about websites and mobile applications.
